What Is PolyFix

Built for industrial demands.

PolyFix is a general-purpose polyester putty formulated for high-adhesion repair on steel and metal surfaces. Developed for professional use in production bodyshops, metal fabrication workshops, and construction environments.

The neutral, unpigmented base accepts any topcoat without bleed-through. Each tin includes the MEKP hardener, pre-measured for consistent results across every application.

How to Apply

Step-by-step guide.

01
Surface Preparation
Sand the repair area to bare metal using 80-grit paper. Remove rust, grease, and contaminants. Clean with a solvent wipe.
02
Mix the Putty
Add 2–3% MEKP hardener by weight to the putty. Mix thoroughly on a clean board for 60–90 seconds until uniform in colour.
03
Apply & Build
Spread with a flexible applicator. Max 6–8mm per layer. For deeper repairs, build in multiple passes, allowing partial cure between coats.
04
Sand & Finish
Once fully cured, block sand with 80, then 180, then 320-grit. The neutral base is ready for primer and topcoat with no bleed-through.
