Use 2–3% hardener by weight at 20°C. Reduce to 1.5–2% in hot conditions and increase to 3–4% in cold conditions to maintain working time.

For standard applications on bare steel, no primer is required. For painted or treated surfaces, light sanding to bare metal is recommended for optimal adhesion.

We recommend a maximum of 6–8mm per layer. For deeper repairs, apply in multiple layers, allowing each to partially cure before adding the next.
